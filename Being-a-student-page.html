Being a Student

        In this page are the moments that I experience as a student.


       Studying   
                 As a student, you will experience being bombarded with activities therefore, time management
                 is a must especially if you're seeking for high grades. Distraction must be avoided and doing
                 your hobbies should not be your priority as it will hinder your studies and it might lead you
                 to fail but you can always get up and face your failure. It is the consequences and challenges
                 that you will face as a student



       Friends   
                 Despite the challenges that you will face at school. You will also gain friends that will be
                 there for you. They will join you through hardships and also with laughters. Exhanging jokes
                 with each other and bond with each other during classes, break time, and at the end of class.
                 You can run to them and ask for help and advice and they will be willing to help you.



       Foods   
                 Food will never be gone in the life of being a student. This is a great bond while being
                 stressed in school. You may eat and enjoy the food with your friends. The food is much more
                 appetizing if it was given by your friend, its free food. It is also the time where you will
                 get to discover other foods that is around your school, and surprisingly affordable rather
                 than the food being sold at canteens.
